Tejo Tungabhadra 
by Vasudhendra

- Hana galisuva vidye namage iradiddare namma tantege indu yaaru baruttiralilla. Hanavilladavaru yaava dharma vannu aacharisutthaare yembudu yaarigu mukhyavaaguvudilla.
- Eshto baari namma pratibhe ginthalu namma maimelina mudre, naama, janivaara, hesaru, sampradaaya, bhaashegalu namage badukannu kattikodalu sahaaya maadutthave.
- "Ee raagi maiya paradeshiyannu sweekarisuvudu biduvudu ninage seride Hampamma. Naanu pandyadalli ondu vele geddaruu appaneyillade yenduu ninna maimuttalaare. Ondu vele sothare, ninna mundina sundara badukina ondu biduvina sanjeyalli nanna hesarannu nene. Ashtu nanage saaku."
- Sannage male shuruvaythu. Bhoomiya gandha sosatodagithu. Anthu malenaadininda mungaaru bayaluseemeyede banditthu. Dhoolininda tumbi hogidda gida maragalu bahudinada nanthara snaana maadi swachhavaagi taleya koodalannu haradikondu neeru tottikkuttiruvanthe kandavu. Jagadella paapagalannu toleyalu ishtu neeru saaladu ennuvanthe male innashtu arbhatisatodagithu.
